Instructions:
1. Click on Data Manager button for import file folder from local computer
2. Then click on User Functions button to chose four different report:
	a) Farm report:
		input farm id and year and click confirm button, 
		display the total milk weight and percent of the total of all farm for each month
	b) Anual report:
		input year and click confirm button, 
		display list of total weight and percent of total weight of all farms by farm for the year
	c) Monthly report:
		input year and month
		display a list of totals and percent of total by farm
	d) Date Range report:
		input start date (year-month-day) and end month-day
		display the total milk weight per farm and the percentage of the total for each farm over that date range.
